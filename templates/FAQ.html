Frequently Asked Questions

Get to know Manisya | Teman Isyarat

Getting Started

What is Manisya ?

Manisya (Teman Isyarat) is an app to translate sign language from camera to text. the app is made by a team of people who are passionate about making a better life for deaf people.

What language is supported ?

Manisya currently supports only Indonesian sign language. In the future, we will be adding more languages. Stay tuned!

How do I use Manisya ?

Just navigate to the manisya page and manisya will automatically translate the sign language to text.

Is Manisya free ?

Yes, Manisya is free. Manisya will be free forever.

Is Manisya secure ?

Manisya will not store any video data on your device. Manisya will only use your camera to translate the sign language to text.

Technical Details

How is Manisya made ?

The apps is made using deep leaning algorithm. This algorithm is based on the idea that the human brain is able to learn from the data.

Is Manisya Open-Source ?

Manisya is open-source. You can find the source code on Github.

How do i report a bug ?

You can report bugs by sending an information via email from the contact page.

What Device is compatible with Manisya ?

Manisya is a web-based application. It is expected to compatible with all devices.

Will manisya make a mobile app ?

Manisya is commited to keep upgrading the application. In the future, we will are going to this app to make it available on all kind of devices.
